### Welcome to DecisionsDev

The DecisionsDev organization hosts open source projects, tools, and samples for **IBM Operational Decision Manager (ODM)**, **IBM Automation Decision Services (ADS)**, **IBM Decision Intelligence (DI)** and related IBM decision automation products.   
Our mission is to provide the community with high-quality resources for working with IBM's decision automation platforms.

### Repository Organization

Our repositories are organized using topics for easy discovery:

- **Products**: `odm`, `decision-intelligence`, `bai`, `cp4ba`
- **Technologies**: `docker`, `kubernetes`, `java`, `react`, `business-rules`
- **Types**: `tutorial`, `sample`, `integration`, `tools`

Use the [repository browser](/repositories) to filter by topics and find exactly what you need. Each repository is tagged with 3-7 relevant topics to help you discover related projects.
